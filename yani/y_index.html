About In Rainbows

Radiohead's 7th studio album, released as a digital download where fans could pay what they wanted. The physical discbox was released on December 3, 2007.

Recorded in English country houses and studios in Oxfordshire and London.

Features the singles "Jigsaw Falling Into Place" and "Nude".

Latest Blog: "About the Release"

Hey everyone,

We're excited to announce our new album "In Rainbows" is available now as a digital download where you can pay whatever you think it's worth. The physical discbox will be out December 3rd with bonus tracks and artwork.

This album was recorded in various locations including a country house in Oxfordshire and our studio in London. We worked with Nigel Godrich as always.

We'll be touring next year - dates coming soon!

- Thom
